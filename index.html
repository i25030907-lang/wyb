🏮 A Day in Tang Dynasty

Xi'an was once the world's largest city under the Tang Dynasty (618–907 AD). Merchants from Persia, scholars from Korea, and monks from India walked its wide boulevards. Today, the city's nightlife at Datang Everbright City recreates that splendor with light shows and hanfu-clad crowds.

Don't miss the Bell Tower at the exact city center – it has witnessed 600 years of history.
